# Historical manifest gap notes

The local archive manifest is calculator-focused. It is suitable for checking
NWAU calculator coverage from 2013-14 onward, but it is not a complete register
of NEP determinations, national pricing model technical specifications, NHCDC
reports, or older cost-weight material.

## Observed local manifest boundary

- Calculator archive evidence starts at 2013-14.
- No 2012-13 NWAU calculator artifact is present in the local manifest.
- The 2012-13 NEP determination and technical specification exist as official
  IHACPA resource pages and downloadable PDFs, but those are specification
  evidence, not calculator evidence.
- NHCDC material should be represented as costing-study evidence, not as NWAU
  calculator parity evidence.

## Follow-on manifest requirements

- Add a separate source-manifest class for NEP determinations and technical
  specifications if the project wants machine-readable provenance beyond the
  calculator archive.
- Add explicit records for standalone price-weight downloads when they exist.
- Add gap records, rather than inferred support, for years where official source
  pages exist but calculator artifacts are absent.
