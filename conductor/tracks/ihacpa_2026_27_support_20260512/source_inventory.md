## Explicit gaps

- No checksums or hashes were captured for any downloaded artefact in this pass.
- The NWAU 2026-27 archive link was confirmed, but the archive contents were not crawled or checksumed here.
- No separate standalone NEC price-weight table file was found; price-weight material is published through the NEP determination and its appendix tables.
- The resource pages themselves do not restate the PDF licence terms inline; licence status was taken from the PDFs where available.
- I did not infer any hidden or unpublished calculator outputs. If a downstream task needs calculator parity, it still needs direct validation against the official 2026-27 calculator files.

## Source notes

- NEP and NEC determinations both refer back to the 2026-27 pricing model and the IHACPA pricing framework.
- The technical-specification PDF is the best provenance source for model methodology and the relationship between NEP, price weights, and adjustments.
- The NWAU calculators page is the best provenance source for operational calculator availability, but it is intentionally conservative about access and hosting details.
